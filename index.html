Communication Made Accessible

Speak with Your Eyes

EyeVoice transforms eye blinks into speech using Morse code. Designed for MND patients and anyone with limited mobility, communicate freely using just your eyes.

How It Works

Simple, intuitive, and accessible communication.

👁️

Blink Detection

Our AI-powered system detects your eye blinks through your webcam in real time.

📷

Morse Code Input

Short blink = dot, long blink = dash. Input letters and words using Morse code.

🔊

Text to Speech

Your message is converted to speech instantly, allowing natural communication.

Our Story

EyeVoice was created with a simple mission: to give a voice to those who have lost the ability to speak.

Using cutting-edge eye-tracking technology and the time-tested Morse code system, EyeVoice bridges the gap between thought and expression.

Because everyone deserves to be heard.
