How to Use
  1. 1
    Click "Start Camera" and position your eyes in the guide oval.
  2. 2
    Short blink (< 500 ms) = dot (.), long blink (500-2000 ms) = dash (-). Wink either eye (300-800ms) to erase single character. 2s close = erase whole word. Morse "SOS" (... --- ...) = emergency help. 8s close = pause/resume. Alternate winks (L-R-L-R) = clear all.
  3. 3
    Wait 1.5 s to confirm a character, 4 s (eyes open, no blinks) for word completion with auto-correction & auto-speech. 6 s pause repeats entire sentence.
  4. 4
    Click "Speak" to hear your message or "Save" to store it.
